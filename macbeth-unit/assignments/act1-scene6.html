Macbeth Act 1 Scene 6 - Classroom Assignment

Story Summary

Setting: Outside Macbeth's castle at Inverness. King Duncan arrives with his retinue.
What Happens: King Duncan, Malcolm, Donalbain, Banquo, Lennox, Macduff, Ross, Angus, and their attendants arrive at Macbeth's castle. Duncan comments on the pleasant air and the castle's welcoming appearance. Lady Macbeth greets them with elaborate courtesy and hospitality, welcoming them to her home. Duncan thanks her and asks to see Macbeth. The scene is filled with dramatic irony as Duncan praises the castle's peacefulness while the audience knows murder is being planned.
Characters: King Duncan, Malcolm (Duncan's eldest son), Donalbain (Duncan's younger son), Banquo, Lennox, Macduff, Ross, Angus, Lady Macbeth, Attendants.
