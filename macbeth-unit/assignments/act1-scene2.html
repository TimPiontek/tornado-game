Macbeth Act 1 Scene 2 - Classroom Assignment

Story Summary

Setting: A camp near Forres, Scotland. King Duncan and his sons are with their army.
What Happens: A wounded captain reports to King Duncan about the battle against the rebel Macdonwald and the Norwegian forces. He describes Macbeth's bravery and victory. Ross arrives with news that the Thane of Cawdor has been defeated and captured. Duncan decides to execute the traitorous Cawdor and give his title to Macbeth as a reward for his valor.
Characters: King Duncan (King of Scotland), Malcolm (Duncan's eldest son), Donalbain (Duncan's younger son), A wounded captain, Ross (a Scottish nobleman), Macbeth (mentioned as a brave warrior), Macdonwald (rebel leader, mentioned), Thane of Cawdor (traitor, mentioned).
