Macbeth Act 1 Scene 1 - Classroom Assignment

Story Summary

Setting: A desolate heath in Scotland, during a thunderstorm. Three witches meet in the opening scene.
What Happens: Three witches gather on a heath during a storm. They discuss their plans to meet Macbeth after a battle. They speak in riddles and prophecies, setting the supernatural tone for the play. They agree to meet again "when the hurlyburly's done" and "when the battle's lost and won."
Characters: Three Witches (also called the Weird Sisters) - supernatural beings who can see the future and manipulate events through their prophecies.
