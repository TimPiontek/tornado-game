How to Play – Tornado War

  1. Create a game: paste student names (one per line), pick a victory %, and click Create game. Each student starts with 5 points by default.
  2. Pick a student in the left roster (click their name). Their color highlights on the map.
  3. Buy Land: click an empty tile to buy it. Cost shows before confirming.
  4. Upgrade Castle: click a tile you own to upgrade its castle (L1→L3) for defense bonuses.
  5. Attack: select an attacker, click an enemy tile, choose mercenaries + weapon level, then resolve. Used mercs/weapons are consumed; if attacker wins, the defender's castle is destroyed.
  6. Truces: students can hold up to 2 active truces. Truced players can't attack each other and can assist each other. Coalitions must capture proportionally more tiles to win.
  7. Award Points: use +1 on a student, inline +/- edits, or Batch Award to many at once.
  8. Buy Tornado: purchase tornado effects for 50 points to randomly affect territories.
  9. Victory: a player (or coalition) wins when they control the target % of tiles (coalitions multiply the target by the number of allied players).

Everything autosaves. Use the Activity panel to see recent actions. You control all actions from this screen; students don't log in.
