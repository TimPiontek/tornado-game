1984 Part 1 - Classroom Assignment

Story Summary

Setting: London, Oceania, in the year 1984. A totalitarian society controlled by the Party, led by Big Brother.
What Happens: Winston Smith, a Party member who works at the Ministry of Truth rewriting historical records, begins to question the Party's control. He buys a diary illegally and starts writing his thoughts. He learns about the three slogans of the Party: "War is Peace, Freedom is Slavery, Ignorance is Strength." He becomes increasingly aware of the surveillance state through telescreens and the Thought Police. He begins to have rebellious thoughts and memories of a different time before the Party's rule.
Characters: Winston Smith (protagonist, Party member), Big Brother (leader figure), O'Brien (Party member), Syme (Winston's colleague), Parsons (Winston's neighbor), Mrs. Parsons (Parsons' wife), Ampleforth (Winston's colleague), Charrington (shopkeeper).
