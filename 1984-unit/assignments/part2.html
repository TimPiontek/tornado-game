1984 Part 2 - Classroom Assignment

Story Summary

Setting: Oceania, 1984. Winston's life takes a dramatic turn as he begins a forbidden relationship and encounters what he believes to be resistance.
What Happens: Winston begins a secret relationship with Julia, a woman from the Fiction Department. They meet in the countryside and rent a room above Mr. Charrington's shop. Winston meets O'Brien, who claims to be a member of the Brotherhood, a resistance movement. O'Brien gives Winston a book called "The Theory and Practice of Oligarchical Collectivism" by Emmanuel Goldstein. Winston and Julia's relationship deepens, but they are eventually betrayed and arrested by the Thought Police.
Characters: Winston Smith (protagonist), Julia (Winston's lover), O'Brien (Party member posing as resistance leader), Mr. Charrington (shopkeeper and Thought Police agent), Emmanuel Goldstein (enemy of the state), Big Brother (leader figure), Syme (Winston's colleague), Parsons (Winston's neighbor).
