Student Tornado War Guide

üéØ Your Mission

Prepare for epic classroom battles! This is your training ground where you build your empire and purchase items for real battles in class.

üè∞ What You Can Do Here

  • Buy territories: Click empty tiles to purchase land (10 coins each)
  • Build castles: Click YOUR territories to build defenses (50/100/200 coins)
  • Purchase weapons: Spear (30), Crossbow (60), Catapult (100 coins)
  • Buy mercenaries: Extra battle power for future fights
  • Get a Wizard: 200 coins to unlock tornado casting

‚öîÔ∏è Classroom Battles

  • When your teacher runs the game in class: You can attack other territories
  • Use your purchased items: Weapons, mercenaries, and tornadoes
  • Defend with castles: Your built castles protect your land
  • Cast tornadoes: If you have a Wizard, spend 200 coins to destroy 2 territories

ü§ù Diplomacy

  • Send truce invitations: Click "Truces" button to manage alliances
  • Accept/decline invitations: Respond to truce requests from classmates
  • Maximum 3 truces: Form strategic alliances with classmates
  • End truces: Break alliances when needed

üí∞ Earning Coins

  • Complete assignments: Your teacher awards coins for good work
  • Spend wisely: Save coins for powerful items like Wizard
  • Your purchases carry over: Everything you buy here is available in class

üèÜ Strategy Tips

  • Build castles on your land: Stronger defense than battle-built castles
  • Purchase weapons before class: Be ready for battles
  • Form strategic alliances: Use truces to protect yourself
  • Save for Wizard: Tornadoes are powerful but expensive

üí° Remember: You can't attack territories here - that's for classroom battles only! This is your preparation zone.
