Romeo & Juliet - Day 2

Act 1, Scenes 2-3: Paris's Proposal & Capulet's Party Plan

üìñ Story Summary

Scene 2: A nobleman asks one of the family patriarchs for his daughter's hand in marriage. The father says his daughter is too young but invites the nobleman to his party that night to woo her. Two young men see the party invitation list, and one convinces the other to attend to see other beautiful women and forget his current love interest.

Scene 3: The mother and a family servant discuss the daughter's potential marriage. The servant shares humorous stories about the daughter's childhood. The daughter says she'll look at the nobleman at the party but won't fall in love unless her parents approve.

Key developments: Marriage proposal, party invitation, a young man's decision to attend, the daughter's introduction
