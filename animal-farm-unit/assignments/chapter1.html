Animal Farm Chapter 1 - Classroom Assignment

Story Summary

Setting: Manor Farm in England, owned by Mr. Jones. The story begins in the barn where the animals gather for a meeting.
What Happens: Old Major, an old boar, calls a meeting of all the animals in the barn. He tells them about a dream he had and shares his vision of a world without humans. He teaches them the song "Beasts of England" and explains the principles of Animalism. He warns them about the cruelty of humans and urges them to rebel. The animals are inspired by his speech and begin to sing the song, waking Mr. Jones who fires his gun into the air to silence them.
Characters: Old Major (old boar, leader), Mr. Jones (farm owner), Boxer (cart-horse), Clover (mare), Benjamin (donkey), Mollie (white mare), Moses (raven), Muriel (goat), Bluebell and Jessie (dogs), and other farm animals.
