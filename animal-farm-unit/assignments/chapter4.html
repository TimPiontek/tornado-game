Animal Farm Chapter 4 - Classroom Assignment

Story Summary

Setting: Animal Farm and neighboring farms. The animals spread news of their rebellion to other farms.
What Happens: News of the Animal Farm rebellion spreads to neighboring farms. The animals send out pigeons to spread the message of rebellion. Mr. Pilkington and Mr. Frederick, the owners of neighboring farms, are initially amused but become concerned about the spread of rebellion. The animals successfully defend their farm against an attack by Mr. Jones and his men in the Battle of the Cowshed. Snowball leads the defense and is wounded. The animals celebrate their victory and establish "Beasts of England" as their anthem.
Characters: Snowball (defense leader), Boxer (brave fighter), Mollie (coward who hides), Mr. Jones (former owner), Mr. Pilkington (neighboring farmer), Mr. Frederick (neighboring farmer), The pigeons (messengers), The sheep (easily confused), Clover (concerned mare), Benjamin (skeptical donkey).
