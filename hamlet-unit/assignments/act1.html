Hamlet Act 1 - Classroom Assignment

Story Summary

Setting: Elsinore Castle in Denmark. The play begins on the battlements of the castle.
What Happens: The ghost of King Hamlet appears to the guards and Horatio. The ghost reveals that he was murdered by his brother Claudius, who has now become king and married Hamlet's mother Gertrude. Hamlet learns of the ghost's appearance and meets with it. The ghost tells Hamlet to avenge his murder but warns him not to harm his mother. Hamlet decides to pretend to be mad to gather information and plan his revenge. Meanwhile, Claudius and Gertrude try to convince Hamlet to stop mourning his father's death.
Characters: Hamlet (Prince of Denmark), King Hamlet (ghost), Claudius (King of Denmark, Hamlet's uncle), Gertrude (Queen of Denmark, Hamlet's mother), Horatio (Hamlet's friend), Rosencrantz and Guildenstern (Hamlet's childhood friends), Polonius (Lord Chamberlain), Ophelia (Polonius's daughter), Laertes (Polonius's son), Marcellus and Barnardo (guards), Francisco (sentry).
